What is Corruption?

Corruption happens when someone in power uses their position for personal gain. This includes bribery, favoritism, theft of public funds, and abuse of authority.

How Corruption Affects Us

Public Services Impact

Loss of government funds meant for schools, roads, healthcare.

Economic Impact

Fewer opportunities for ordinary citizens and small businesses.

Social Impact

Distrust in government and institutions.

Poverty Impact

Poverty becomes harder to escape when corruption is present.

The Importance of Integrity

Integrity means doing the right thing even when no one is watching. A society built on integrity is safer, fairer, and more progressive.

Know Your Rights

Filipinos have the right to transparent governance, access to information, and protection against abuse of power.

Who We Are

Project PINOY is a student-led advocacy group dedicated to promoting transparency, honesty, and accountability
in everyday life. We believe that the fight against corruption begins with awareness, education, and individual action.

Our Mission

To inspire Filipinos—especially the youth—to recognize corruption, reject dishonest practices, and encourage a culture of integrity.

Our Vision

A Philippines where every citizen, office, and institution values honesty, fairness, and justice.

What We Do

  • Create educational materials on corruption.
  • Promote youth involvement in community awareness.
  • Encourage ethical behavior inside and outside school.
  • Provide space where students can learn and speak up.

Our Team

A group of students committed to making a positive change through advocacy, research, and community engagement.
